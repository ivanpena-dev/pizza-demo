About Bella’s

A mom-and-pop shop where every pizza is made with love, care, and attention to detail.

At Lil’ Bella’s Pizza, we’re proud to be a mom-and-pop shop, where every pizza is made with love, care, and attention to detail. It all starts with our handmade dough, prepared fresh daily right here in our kitchen. Next, we add our signature pizza sauce — a rich, flavorful blend that’s thicker and more robust than what you’ll find at other pizzerias.

We top our pizzas with only the freshest vegetables and premium meats, ensuring you can taste the quality in every bite. And then there’s the cheese — 100% real Wisconsin mozzarella. It’s one of the finest (and yes, most expensive) cheeses available, but we believe you’re worth the very best.

Finally, we bake your pizza to perfection in our traditional deck oven, letting the flavors and seasonings mingle as they cook slowly to golden, bubbling perfection. We don’t believe in rushing a good thing — and we think you’ll agree it’s worth the wait.

If you’re looking for a fast, flavorless pizza, you might not be the right fit. But if you want a handcrafted pizza that’s big on taste and made with love, we’d be thrilled to serve you.
